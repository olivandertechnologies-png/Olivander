DATABASE SCHEMA
================================================================================

Tables (5 total):
  - businesses (OAuth tokens, business info)
  - approvals (email drafts pending approval)
  - memory (key-value business context)
  - activity (audit log)
  - oauth_states (temporary OAuth flow state)

Indexes (6 total):
  - approvals (business_id, status)
  - approvals (created_at DESC)
  - memory (business_id, key)
  - activity (business_id, created_at DESC)
  - oauth_states (expires_at)
  - businesses (email)

RLS Policies:
  - Enforced on all business-related tables
  - Users can only see their own data
