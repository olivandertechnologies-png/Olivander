Olivander

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026  ·  Applies to olivander.vercel.app

Olivander is an AI operations layer for small service businesses in New Zealand. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

The short version: we access your Gmail, Calendar, and Xero accounts only to do the work you've asked us to do. We never read emails to sell ads, train our own models, or share data with third parties beyond the services listed below. Nothing is sent or actioned without your explicit approval.

1. What we collect

When you connect your accounts, we access:

  • Gmail — inbound email content and thread history, to classify messages and draft replies; recent sent mail, to extract your writing style during voice calibration
  • Google Calendar — your event list and availability, to handle booking requests
  • Xero — contacts and invoice data, to create and chase invoices on your behalf

We also store the business information you provide during onboarding (business name, location, pricing, policies) and a log of all actions Olivander has taken or queued.

Your browser's local storage is used to hold your session token and UI preferences (e.g. theme). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers beyond authentication.

2. How we use it

  • Classify inbound emails and identify what response or action is needed
  • Draft replies and propose calendar slots for your review
  • Create invoices and send follow-up chasers after you approve them
  • Extract a compact writing profile from recent sent emails so drafts sound closer to you
  • Remember your business preferences so responses stay consistent

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics products, or to train AI models. We take reasonable steps to ensure information we hold about you is accurate and up to date.

3. AI processing and Google API data use

Email content, calendar details, and business memory are sent to Groq (running Meta's Llama 3.3 70B model) to generate classifications and draft text. For voice calibration, a bounded recent sample of your sent mail is also sent to Groq to extract style only. Groq processes this data under their own privacy policy. We send only what's needed for each operation — we do not bulk-export your inbox to any AI service.

All AI-generated outputs are drafts. Nothing is sent or actioned until you approve it.

Google API Limited Use: Olivander's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. In plain terms: Gmail and Calendar data is used only to provide the features described in this policy. It is not used for advertising, not transferred to third parties for their own use, and no human at Olivander reads your email content unless you explicitly request support and provide access.

4. Third-party data in emails

When your customers and contacts email you, their messages may contain personal information about them (names, phone numbers, booking details). This content is processed by Groq as part of drafting replies on your behalf. By using Olivander, you represent that you have a lawful basis for processing your contacts' information in this way, consistent with your own privacy obligations to them.

5. Who else sees your data

We use the following third-party services to operate Olivander:

  • Supabase — database hosting (PostgreSQL, hosted in AWS ap-southeast-2, Sydney)
  • Google — Gmail and Calendar API access
  • Xero — accounting API access
  • Groq — AI inference (United States)
  • Render — backend API hosting (United States)
  • Vercel — frontend hosting (United States)

We do not sell data to any third party. We do not share data with marketers, data brokers, or analytics services.

Cross-border transfers: Some of the services above are based in the United States. Under Information Privacy Principle 12 of the NZ Privacy Act 2020, we transfer personal information overseas where it is necessary to perform the service you have contracted us to provide, and we take reasonable steps to ensure these overseas recipients handle your information in a way that is consistent with the Privacy Act. By using Olivander you acknowledge and consent to these transfers.

6. Data retention

  • Email content used for drafting is not stored long-term — it's fetched, processed, and discarded
  • Raw sent emails used for voice calibration are not stored long-term — only the compact writing profile and owner-approved example are retained
  • Approval records and activity logs are retained for as long as your account is active
  • Business memory (your preferences, policies) is retained and can be edited or deleted by you at any time via the Memory panel
  • On account deletion, all data is removed within 30 days

7. Security

OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256. All API communication is over HTTPS. Row-level security is enforced at the database layer — one business cannot access another's data.

8. Privacy breach notification

If we become aware of a privacy breach that is likely to cause serious harm to any affected individual, we will notify the NZ Privacy Commissioner and the affected individuals as soon as practicable, in accordance with Part 6 of the Privacy Act 2020. We will also take immediate steps to contain the breach and assess its scope.

9. Your rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and close your account at any time
  • Make a complaint to the NZ Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz if you believe we have breached the Privacy Act

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ollieblakey85@gmail.com. We will respond within 20 working days as required by the Act.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll notify you by email before they take effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
